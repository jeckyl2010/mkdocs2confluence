That is useful because the protocols, trust boundaries, and operational constraints are different. Keycloak gives us one consistent entry point without pretending those systems are the same thing.

!!! note "Scope"
    This page explains the value of **Keycloak** as a site-local front door running on the local **OpenShift** platform. It does not change the control boundaries described above.
